Our Owners

Mohammad Farhad

Mohammad Farhad is a founder of Indopak Supermarket and India Import. He wanted to serve his community by offering affordable groceries along with a taste of home. After over two decades, this is still his goal. Indopak has grown tremendously and now has multiple supermarkets, butchers, cafes and a banquet hall. Mohammad Farhad is married with two children.

MD Shawkat Ali

MD Shawkat Ali is a founder of Indopak Supermarket and India Import. Shawkat Ali came to the US as a student when he was 18 years old. He realised that the Asian grocery business had great potential in the DFW area. Since then, six supermarkets have been created in six cities throughout the Dallas-Forth Worth metroplex. MD Shawkat Ali is married and has two wonderful children.
